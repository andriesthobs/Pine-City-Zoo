WARTHOG

Warthogs (Phacochoerus africanus) are wild pigs found in sub-Saharan Africa. Known for their distinctive facial warts and long tusks, they inhabit open and semi-open habitats, including savannas and grasslands. They are mostly herbivorous but sometimes consume small animals.

Warthogs are tough, adaptable creatures and typically live in burrows for protection.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM?

You will find the warthogs at WA11, near the elephant enclosure.
