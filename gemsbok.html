GEMSBOK

The gemsbok (Oryx gazella) is a large antelope native to the arid regions of Southern Africa like the Kalahari Desert. They are known for their long, straight horns and distinctive facial markings.

The gemsbok is a symbol of Namibia, appearing on its coat of arms. Though it resembles the chamois, it is unrelated.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM?

You will find the gemsboks just beyond Panda Canyon.
