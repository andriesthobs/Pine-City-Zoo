GORILLA

Gorillas are the largest living primates, found in the forests of Central Africa. Divided into eastern and western species, they live in tropical forests and range in elevation from sea level to 4,300 metres. Gorillas are closely related to humans, sharing up to 99% of our DNA.

They are ground-dwelling herbivores, typically living in family groups led by a dominant silverback male.
